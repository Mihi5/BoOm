Hello and welcome on boom-music.eu!

... a fun and free to use website for creating and playing music.

  • This site was originally inspired by Boomwhackers but can be used for creating and playing practically every kind of music.
  • We have some predefined melodies to use and play which you can edit and adapt to your liking.
  • You can even create Your own composition - just click on the pencil icon in the drop-down menu by the player: , then adjust the settings to Your wishes and write Your song.
  • When ready You can download Your arrangement by clicking on the download icon: and save the file on Your computer.
  • At any time You can load and use Your saved melody by clicking on the upload:
  • Currently, the only limitation is that Your arrangement has to be with no more than 4 voices.
  • If You like to see Your song included on this website You can send us a note.
